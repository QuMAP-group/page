Beyond WIMPs Conference IV

March 23–26, 2026 · Penthouse, Department of Physics, University of Liverpool

About the Event

Dark Matter Beyond the Weak Scale IV (BeyondWIMPs IV) returns to Liverpool for the fourth instalment of the workshop series. The meeting brings together theorists and experimentalists working on dark matter beyond the traditional weak-scale WIMP paradigm, with a focus on building connections across new ideas, models, and emerging detection strategies.

For the full conference webpage, visit beyondwimps.com. For the scientific programme and event details, see the Vol. 4 conference programme.
