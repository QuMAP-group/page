QUEST-DMC Workshop

Oct 15–17, 2025 · Penthouse, Department of Physics, University of Liverpool

About the Event

The meeting brings together researchers exploring how superfluid helium can reveal the tiniest traces of dark matter - and how phase transitions in quantum fluids mirror those that shaped the early universe. Talks, discussions, and strategy sessions will fill three inspiring days overlooking Liverpool, uniting expertise from across the collaboration

Details

  • Date: October 15–17, 2025
  • Time: 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM
  • Location: Penthouse, Department of Physics, University of Liverpool
